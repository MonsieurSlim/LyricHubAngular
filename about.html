About Lyric Hub

- A user can search for a song based on title and artist name.

- The user can then use the results to search for the song lyrics.

- To get lyrics, both song title and artist name have to be supplied.

- Users can play any of the songs that appear in the search results area and listen to them without leaving the app.

- The link to the full lyrics opens in another tab. This ensures that the player on the app keeps playing while the user looks through the lyrics page, and sings along.
